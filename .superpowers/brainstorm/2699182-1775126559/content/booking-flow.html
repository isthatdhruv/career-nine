Cancellation & Reschedule Rules

🚫 4-Hour Rule

No cancellations within 4 hours of session start time. Enforced server-side for all roles.

🔄 Reschedule

Counsellor proposes new slot directly. Old appointment → RESCHEDULED. New appointment created and auto-CONFIRMED. Audit log captures both.

📧 Notifications

Every cancel/reschedule triggers email to all other parties + in-app notification. Audit log records who did it and why.
