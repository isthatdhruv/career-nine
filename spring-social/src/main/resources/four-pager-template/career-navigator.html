About Career-9

Career-9 redefines career selection for an AI-driven world by viewing it as a continuous journey from Grade 6 to 12 rather than a one-time test. Our flagship tool, Navigator 360, uses a research-backed 6-pillar framework—endorsed by leading psychologists—to align a student's innate potential with their evolving preferences. By uncovering hidden strengths and linking them with passions, we empower students to own their journey with clarity and confidence.

Student Career Action Flow
1
Explore Pathways
  • Look at career websites and videos for your top suited fields
  • Visit colleges, design institutes, or labs if possible
  • Talk to people in similar professions (alumni, professionals)
2
Discuss and Shortlist
  • Talk to family, teachers, mentors, and friends about your goals
  • Discuss what excites you and what feels realistic
  • Shortlist 1 to 3 career options from your top suited careers
3
Set Goals and Plan
  • Choose one direction for preparation and focus
  • Set short-term goals for academics this year
  • Make a simple monthly action plan with weekly milestones
4
Make Notes and Track
  • Keep a notebook or digital file for your career journey
  • Write what you learned, liked, and disliked each month
  • Track tasks, deadlines, and progress against your goals
5
Check Your Fit
  • Ask: Do I still enjoy this path after a few months?
  • Notice your energy, interest, and confidence in subjects
  • See what feels natural and meaningful vs. forced
6
Reflect and Respond
  • Identify problems or confusion; discuss with counsellor
  • Either go back to Step 1 and explore again if needed
  • Or build strong habits and take preparation courses
7
Review After 1 Year

Recheck your progress, interests, and skills after 12 months

Compare growth with your earlier action plan milestones

Map yourself again and update the journey with counsellor
